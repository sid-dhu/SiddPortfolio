EXTRA TERRESTRIAL

The project began with the idea of exploring a narrative set in a post-apocalyptic ice age era, but not on Earth.
Instead, I envisioned a story unfolding on an exoplanet named Planet XZ-345,
inhabited by an intelligent species eerily similar to humans.

This species thrived and ultimately faced extinction due to their own mistakes,
leading to a catastrophic ice age. This narrative aims to raise awareness about environmental conservation
and the preservation of Earth's natural resources.
