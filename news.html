Man Claims He Can Talk to Pigeons; Pigeons Refuse to Comment

A man in Manchester has sparked curiosity by claiming he can communicate with pigeons, but the birds themselves remained tight-lipped, continuing their usual cooing without any additional commentary. Yusuf Dikec, a Turkish shooter, has taken an unexpected turn during the 2024 Summer Olympics in Paris and gained widespread attention online.
